Whether you have been thinking about it for weeks or made the decision in a moment of quiet clarity, arriving prepared helps you get the most from the experience. This guide walks you through everything you need to know before your first session at Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness.

Step 2: Why Sessions Are via Chat or Voice Call — Not Video

All sessions at Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness are conducted through private digital communication — chat, voice notes, or a scheduled audio call. Video call sessions are not offered, and this is an intentional, considered decision rooted in both the integrity of the practice and the quality of your experience.

Why This Matters for You

The quality of what you receive in a tarot, rune, or energy wellness session depends entirely on the practitioner's ability to maintain deep, undistracted focus. When clients observe cards or ritual materials on video in real time, natural human reactions — leaning forward, asking mid-draw questions, visible expressions — can subtly interrupt the practitioner's energetic connection and shift the guidance toward what the client's visible reaction suggests they want to hear, rather than what the cards or symbols are genuinely indicating. Chat and voice protect the integrity and accuracy of what you receive.

Step 3: What You Receive After Your Session

  • For tarot and rune sessions: written insights, reflections, and symbolic interpretations shared through your chosen digital channel after the session is complete.
  • For energy wellness, crystal alignment, and ritual sessions: you receive a photograph of the completed session arrangement alongside a comprehensive written analysis.

Your Written Analysis Includes

  • An explanation of the specific crystals, symbols, or elements used and why they were selected for your intention
  • An interpretation of what the session revealed about your current energetic situation
  • Personalised guidance and reflections drawn from the session
  • Practical suggestions for the days that follow

Step 4: Prepare Yourself — Practically and Energetically

  • Create quiet around the time of your session — give yourself space to be present rather than rushed
  • Come with an open mind — the most transformative sessions are often the ones that offer unexpected perspective
  • Write down your intention or question beforehand — even a few private words help clarify and settle your energy
  • Avoid alcohol for a few hours before your session — a clear and settled mind receives guidance more fully
  • Give yourself time after the session — leave an hour or two to simply be with whatever has come up
  • No materials needed — no crystals, candles, cards, or equipment required. Your own home is perfectly appropriate

Step 5: After Your Session — How to Integrate What You Receive

  • Read your analysis slowly and more than once — things that seem less significant on first reading often become the most resonant insights the next day
  • Journal what comes up — ten minutes of free writing after your session anchors insights in a way that thinking alone does not
  • Act on one thing — choose one small, concrete shift and commit to it in the week following your session
  • Notice what shifts in the days that follow — clients often report that the most significant effects emerge three to seven days after a session

"A wellness session is an investment in your own clarity, wellbeing, and inner direction. The more genuinely you engage with it, the more it offers in return."

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are sessions via chat or voice call, not video?
Sessions are conducted through chat, voice notes, or audio call to protect the practitioner's focused presence and the integrity of the session. Video can introduce reactions, interruptions, technical issues, and environmental distractions that reduce session quality.
What do I receive after an energy wellness session?
After an energy wellness, crystal alignment, or ritual session, clients receive a photograph of the completed arrangement and a written analysis explaining the elements used, the intention behind them, what the session revealed, and practical guidance for the days ahead.
Do I need any materials for my session?
No. Clients do not need crystals, candles, cards, or equipment. Sessions are practitioner-led and conducted online.
How should I prepare for my first session?
Complete the pre-session questionnaire honestly, create quiet around the session time, write down your question or intention, come with an open mind, avoid alcohol for a few hours beforehand, and leave time afterward to reflect.
How do I book my first session?
Book through the Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness booking page, select the appropriate service, complete the booking form, and confirm payment. Clients can also reach out through WhatsApp or Instagram for help choosing the right starting point.
