What Is Energy Healing?

Energy healing is a broad term for a category of complementary wellness practices rooted in the understanding that our emotional states, mental patterns, stress responses, and sense of inner balance are influenced by subtler dimensions of our experience that conventional medicine does not always address.

Different energy healing trAditi - Spiritual Practitionerons approach this in different ways. What they share is a focus on restoring a felt sense of balance, calm, and inner alignment — not through physical intervention, but through focused intention, symbolic practice, and mindful presence.

At Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness, energy wellness sessions are practitioner-led online experiences that incorporate crystal and candle wellness practices within a calm, reflective framework. Sessions are conducted entirely online through private digital communication.

How Does an Online Energy Wellness Session Work?

The most common question from first-time clients is: how can energy healing work online if the practitioner is not physically present? Energy wellness is not a physically hands-on practice — it is a practice of focused intention, symbolic work, and mindful presence. These do not require physical proximity.

1

Booking and Intention-Setting

After booking, you receive a pre-session questionnaire about your chosen area of focus — what feels out of balance, or what you want to invite more of into your life.

2

The Session

Aditi - Spiritual Practitioner conducts the energy wellness session on your behalf using selected crystals and candle practices with your intention held at the centre. You do not need to do anything during this time.

3

What You Receive

After the session, you receive a photograph of the completed crystal and candle arrangement alongside a detailed written analysis — explaining what was used, why, what the session revealed, and personalised guidance for the days ahead.

"Video recordings of wellness practices are not shared because the work is energetically specific to each client and conducted in a private, protected space. What you receive instead is a complete, considered, and deeply personalised account of what was done and what it means."
