How to Choose the Right
Online Wellness Session for You

By Aditi · Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness 12 min read

One of the most common questions before booking a first session is simple: which one is right for me? If you have found yourself drawn to the services at Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness but unsure where to begin, this guide walks through each offering and helps you identify the most natural fit based on what you are seeking right now.

There is no wrong choice. Every session is personalised to your individual focus and intentions. But understanding what each offers helps you arrive with clarity and get the most from your experience.

Start Here: What Are You Seeking Right Now?

Before comparing sessions, consider which of the following resonates most:

  • I have a specific question or decision I need clarity on — a relationship situation, a career crossroads, a life choice I cannot resolve on my own
  • I am going through a period of change or transition and want reflective support and perspective
  • I feel emotionally drained, ungrounded, or out of balance and want to restore a sense of calm
  • I want to set clear intentions and align my energy around a goal, new beginning, or significant life shift
  • I am simply curious and want to explore symbolic wellness in a private, professional setting

Your answer naturally points you toward one of the sessions below.

Session 1: Intuitive Tarot Guidance

Most Requested

Intuitive Tarot Guidance

Best for: Clarity on a specific question, decision, or area of life

Tarot guidance is the most requested session at Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness and the most versatile starting point. It works whether you have a very specific question in mind or simply want a broad reflective overview of where you are right now.

Choose this session if:

  • You have a question about a relationship, career move, or life decision to explore
  • You are at a crossroads and want symbolic perspective on your options
  • You feel stuck in a pattern and want a reflective lens on why
  • You are new to tarot and want an accessible, welcoming introduction
  • You want a session that can cover multiple areas of your life in one sitting

Session 2: Rune Symbol Guidance

Ancient Nordic Tradition

Rune Symbol Guidance

Best for: Personal strength, major transitions, and inner direction

Rune guidance is particularly resonant for those drawn to ancient traditions, navigating significant personal transitions, or who prefer a more focused archetypal framework.

Choose this session if:

  • You are drawn to Nordic or ancient symbolic traditions
  • You are going through a significant life transition — relationship, career, home, or personal identity
  • You have tried tarot before and want to explore a different symbolic modality
  • You are interested in themes of personal strength, resilience, and inner direction

Session 3: Energy Wellness Sessions

Practitioner-Led

Energy Wellness Sessions

Best for: Restoring balance, calm, and emotional grounding

Energy wellness sessions are for those who feel emotionally depleted, mentally scattered, or out of alignment — and want a session focused less on questions and more on restoration.

Choose this session if:

  • You are feeling emotionally exhausted, anxious, or out of balance
  • You want a session focused on rest, grounding, and inner calm rather than analytical reflection
  • You are curious about crystal and candle wellness practices in a guided professional context
  • You want a complement to your existing mindfulness or self-care routine

Session 4: Soul Alignment & Wellness Sessions

New Moon · Full Moon · Seasonal

Soul Alignment & Wellness Sessions

Best for: New beginnings, intention-setting, and conscious release

These sessions are for those who want to use significant timing — a new moon, full moon, Ekadashi, or seasonal transition — as a conscious anchor for setting direction and releasing what no longer serves them.

Choose this session if:

  • You want to set clear, conscious intentions around a goal or new beginning
  • You feel drawn to working with lunar cycles — New Moon for planting, Full Moon for releasing
  • You are starting something new and want to approach it with deliberate intention
  • You want a session that combines reflective guidance with a sense of ritual and meaning

What All Sessions Have in Common

Regardless of which session you choose, every experience at Infinite Soul Awakening Wellness shares the same foundations:

  • Fully online and private — chat, voice notes, or audio call with no video required
  • No prior knowledge needed — every session is accessible to complete beginners
  • Personalised to you — no session follows a generic script
  • Practitioner-led — you do not need to perform any practices or have any materials at home
  • Honest and grounded — no guaranteed outcomes are promised

"The right session is not the one that sounds most impressive — it is the one that matches what you actually need right now. When in doubt, start with tarot. It is the most versatile first step."

Frequently Asked Questions

Which session should I choose as a first-time client?
For most first-time clients, Intuitive Tarot Guidance is the most versatile starting point because it can cover relationships, career, decisions, life transitions, and general clarity in one session.
What is the difference between tarot and rune guidance?
Tarot uses a 78-card symbolic system and is especially useful for specific questions, relationships, career decisions, and life direction. Rune guidance uses ancient Nordic symbols and is especially suited to transitions, personal strength, resilience, and inner direction.
What session is best for emotional balance and stress?
Energy Wellness Sessions are best suited for emotional balance, grounding, calm, and restoration. They are practitioner-led and may incorporate crystal and candle wellness practices.
Can I switch between sessions?
Yes. Clients can book different sessions at different times depending on what they are navigating. Many clients begin with tarot and later explore rune guidance, energy wellness, or soul alignment sessions.
